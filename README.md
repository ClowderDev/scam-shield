# Project Title

ScamShield – Decentralized Insurance for Online Scam Protection on Stellar

## Project Vision
ScamShield aims to become a **trustless safety layer for digital transactions**, where users are no longer fully exposed to the risks of scams in decentralized environments.  

The long-term vision is to build a **community-driven insurance ecosystem** that:
- Protects users from financial losses caused by scams  
- Leverages decentralized governance (DAO) for fair decision-making  
- Uses transparent, on-chain data to ensure accountability  
- Evolves into a sustainable DeFi insurance protocol with real risk modeling  

Ultimately, ScamShield strives to make blockchain adoption safer by **reducing fear and increasing user confidence** in digital finance.

## Description
Online scams are increasing rapidly, especially in crypto and digital payments where transactions are irreversible. Victims often have no protection or way to recover lost funds.

This project aims to create a decentralized insurance system where users can purchase protection against scams. If a user becomes a victim, they can submit a claim, and the system (via DAO voting or predefined rules) determines whether compensation should be issued.

The goal is to combine blockchain transparency + community governance to build a fair and trustless safety net.

## Features

### 1. Buy Insurance Policy
- Users can purchase a policy by paying a premium  
- Policy includes:
  - Coverage amount (max payout)  
  - Ownership tied to wallet address  
- Stored on-chain for transparency  

---

### 2. Submit Scam Claim
- Insured users can submit a claim if they are scammed  
- Claim includes:
  - Requested payout amount  
  - Linked to claimant wallet  
- Stored on-chain as a proposal  

---

### 3. DAO-based Claim Voting
- Community (or token holders) vote on claims  
- Each claim tracks:
  - Votes for / against  
- Prevents centralized control  

---

### 4. Claim Resolution
- Claims are approved if `votes_for > votes_against`  
- Decision is transparent and immutable  

---

### 5. Payout Mechanism (Prototype Stage)
- Approved claims can trigger payout logic  
- Current version: logic placeholder  
- Future: integrate token transfer / liquidity pool  

---

### 6. On-chain Data Transparency
- Policies and claims stored in smart contract  
- Anyone can verify:
  - Who bought insurance  
  - Who submitted claims  
  - Voting results
